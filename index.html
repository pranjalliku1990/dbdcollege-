Welcome to Dakshin Bijni Degree College

Dakshin Bijni Degree College is a premier educational institution located in the heart of Dakshin Bijni, Assam. Established in 1985, the college has a long and proud history of providing quality education to students from all walks of life.

About Dakshin Bijni Degree College

Dakshin Bijni Degree College is a government-aided college affiliated with Gauhati University. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in the arts, science, and commerce streams. The college has a dedicated and experienced faculty who are committed to providing students with a high-quality education.

The college has a strong focus on extracurricular activities and sports. It has a well-equipped library, a computer lab, a gymnasium, and a playground. The college also organizes various cultural and sports events throughout the year.

Courses Offered

  • Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)
  • Bachelor of Arts (B.A) honors

Admissions

Admissions to Dakshin Bijni Degree College are open to students who have passed the Higher Secondary (10+2) examination with a minimum of 50% marks. Students can apply for admission online or in person. The admission process typically begins in May and ends in August.
